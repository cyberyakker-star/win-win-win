Odds & Return to Player

The RTPs listed are based on the optimal strategy for Let It Ride.

Let It Ride 96.49%
Three Card Bonus 95.80%

Return to Player (RTP) is the long-run share of all wagers paid back as winnings. The house keeps the remainder on average, which is how the vault stays funded for future players. Three Card Bonus RTP here is 95.80% — slightly better than the 95.62% found on some commercial versions of this game.

The chances of getting a particular outcome are always the same at the start of every game. Cards are dealt from a freshly shuffled 52-card deck each hand using a cryptographically seeded shuffle.

Optimal strategy (used for the RTP above)

  • With 3 cards, let it ride with: any paying hand (pair of 10s+ or trips); three to a royal flush; three suited cards in a row except A-2-3 and 2-3-4; three to a straight flush spread 4 with at least one card 10 or higher; three to a straight flush spread 5 with at least two cards 10 or higher.
  • With 4 cards, let it ride with: any paying hand; four to a flush; four to an outside straight with at least one card 10 or higher; four to any straight with four cards 10 or higher.
  • Otherwise, pull the bet back.
